我是橙子.
我是一名动态设计师和二维动画师.

I was born in 1983 and grew up in a little town in the middle of nowhere in surrounded by the Italian Alps, spending time building spaceships with Legos and dreaming of becoming a paleontologist.

Things went differently then expected: I graduated from The University of Milan with a degree in Audio-Visual Communications and in 2011 I started my career as freelance motion designer, that brought me to work for world-renowned brands such as Google, MTV, NBC, Uefa, Espn, Microsoft, Toyota, Ibm and American Express.

I am currently working at Antimatter, a high-end animation studio as a designer and creative director.
